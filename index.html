Guozhen (Gordon) Ji

I am an empirical IO economist and an incoming Associate at Econic Partners.

My research sits at the intersection of structural economics and public policy, with a specific focus on optimal pricing in the environmental and transportation sectors. I specialize in applying structural IO methods, causal inference, and machine learning techniques to massive datasets to solve complex liability, pricing, and antitrust problems.

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics from the University of Texas at Austin and hold a B.S. in Statistics and Economics from the University of Chicago.
